Visiting Hyde

Directions to Hyde Memorial Observatory

Hyde Memorial Observatory is located on the south side of Holmes Lake in Southeast Lincoln Nebraska. From downtown take Normal Boulevard all the way to South 70th Street, then turn south (right) and continue past the North Shore Road entrance to the South Shore Road entrance to Holmes Park. This is the same entrance used to get to the golf course. Turn west (right) onto the South Shore Road and continue past the golf course turnoff until you see the observatory on your left

Special Arrangements

Special arrangements can be mad for groups of 20 or more (clubs, church, groups, classes or even just a group of friends) on nights other than the regularly scheduled Saturday nights. here is how Hyde can help: The observatory can be opened for specials groups. Observatory staff can sometimes visit your group to give a presentation of sky tour. To make special arrangements call 1–402–441–7895 (weekdays 8:30am & 5:00pm) at least two weeks in advance. there is no charge, although a small honorarium may be requested for visiting speakers.

Open Most Saturdays! Free Admission!

Please Note: The South Shore of Holmes Lake normally closes at 6pm; however it will be kept open on those nights when Hyde Observatory is open, for Hyde Observatory patrons only. For the current programs and schedules: Call (402)–7094.

For more information call the Lincoln Parks and Recreation Dept. at 441–8708

Current Hours: October–March 7pm to 10pm. Open every Saturday.
